About



One Direction, often shortened to 1D,
are an English-Irish pop boy band formed in London in 2010.

The group is composed of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson,
and previously Zayn Malik until his
departure from the group in March 2015.
